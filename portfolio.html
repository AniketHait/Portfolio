Smart City Coverage Using Wireless 5G Networks

In this project I tried to make a group of cars in a single environment in which only the first car will have a driver,and other cars will follow the header accordingly. The other cars will ask for information from the header(info related to the road situation eg traffics,jam etc) and accordingly will move on. We will be creating base station along the road also in which a receiver and transmitter will be there to relay information to each team of cars.

It was published on IEEE (CALCON) on 9th dec,2022
